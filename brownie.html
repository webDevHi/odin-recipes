BROWNIES!!

DESCRIPTION

Brownies are a delectable treat enjoyed by cultures across the world. Brownies are sweet chocalate flour-based deserts that pack on the calories and the flavor! Chocalate chips, icings, even yogurt can be used to spice up brownies easily, making them one of the most versatile basics in the culinary desert world

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A bit of butter
  • A smidgem of cocoa
  • A couple eggs
  • A metric duck-ton of sugar
  • a pinch of salt
  • chocalate chunks

STEPS

  1. Preheat Oven to 325 farenheit
  2. Chopp up chocalate into desired consistency and add to bowl
  3. Add other ingredients into bowl
  4. mix with mixer
  5. bake for 40 minutes
