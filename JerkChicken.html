JERK CHICKEN

DESCRIPTION

A staple of Jamaican dishes, Jerk Chicken is a time consuming yet delicous and hardy meal. The star of the dish is usually the Jerk seasoning, a salty brine with an exilerating kick that is sure to make anyone's taste buds sing in delight. Pair a bit of lime and rice with this gorgeous chicken to get a soul-filling, worth-while dish every time!
